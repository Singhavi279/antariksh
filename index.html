Why अंतरिक्ष

India's Astrology Ecosystem Is Thriving — But Fragmented

The expertise exists. The audience is growing. What's missing is a trusted, institutional platform that brings it all together.

🛡️

A Trust Gap at the Centre

The market is growing rapidly, but unverified practitioners and inconsistent quality have made it difficult for genuine seekers to find credible experts. Trust is the sector's biggest unsolved problem.

🔗

A Fragmented Ecosystem

Astrologers, Vastu masters, Tarot readers, and numerologists work in parallel universes with no shared platform for collaboration, peer learning, or industry standards.

👤

A Missing Physical Layer

A sector that thrives on personal guidance and human trust has moved almost entirely digital — losing the high-trust, face-to-face interactions that practitioners and seekers both value most.

📖

No Structured Knowledge Transfer

Deep Vedic knowledge is being displaced by short-form content. There is no institutional mechanism for advanced practitioners to mentor emerging talent.

Process

How Nominations Are Evaluated

1

Nomination Submission

Nominees apply online, pay the nomination fee and submit their profile, credentials, supporting documents and work samples.

2

Eligibility Screening

The internal awards team reviews submissions for completeness, eligibility and category fit before forwarding to the jury.

3

Independent Jury Scoring

Three independent expert jurors score each eligible nominee out of 100 points. Scores are submitted independently — no juror sees another's score.

4

Score Normalisation & Review

The three scores are averaged. Significant outliers are reviewed. Conflicted scores are removed and replaced.

5

Winner Announcement

The final winner list is sealed, signed off by the Awards Director and Chief Jury, and announced live on stage at the ceremony.

Evaluation Criteria

Knowledge & Expertise Impact & Contribution Client & Community Trust Digital Presence & Content Innovation & Growth Business Excellence Ethical Practice

The jury's decision is final and binding. Awards recognise excellence, contribution and integrity — not popularity or commercial volume.
