Process

1

Nomination Submission

Nominees apply online, pay the nomination fee and submit their profile, credentials, supporting documents and work samples.

2

Eligibility Screening

The internal awards team reviews submissions for completeness, eligibility and category fit before forwarding to the jury.

3

Independent Jury Scoring

Three independent expert jurors score each eligible nominee out of 100 points. Scores are submitted independently — no juror sees another's score.

4

Score Normalisation & Review

The three scores are averaged. Significant outliers are reviewed. Conflicted scores are removed and replaced.

5

Winner Announcement

The final winner list is sealed, signed off by the Awards Director and Chief Jury, and announced live on stage at the ceremony.

Evaluation Criteria

Knowledge & Expertise Impact & Contribution Client & Community Trust Digital Presence & Content Innovation & Growth Business Excellence Ethical Practice

The jury's decision is final and binding. Awards recognise excellence, contribution and integrity — not popularity or commercial volume.

Fellowship

Guru–Shishya Fellowship

Reviving India's oldest pedagogical tradition — connecting masters of Vedic sciences with the next generation of dedicated seekers.

Fellowship Synopsis

The Guru–Shishya Fellowship at Astro+ महासंगम is a structured mentorship programme rooted in the classical Indian tradition of knowledge transfer. It bridges verified Gurus — seasoned practitioners across astrology, Vastu, tarot, numerology, and palmistry — with committed Shishyas who seek authentic, in-depth learning beyond what digital platforms can offer.

The fellowship is designed for depth, not breadth. A Guru accepts a limited number of Shishyas each cycle; a Shishya commits to a defined period of structured learning. Both are verified, both are accountable.

Verified Gurus

Practitioners with a demonstrated body of work, peer recognition, and a commitment to structured knowledge transmission.

Structured Cycles

Time-bound, outcome-oriented fellowship cycles — not open-ended arrangements. Clear expectations on both sides.

Vedic Disciplines

Covering astrology, Vastu Shastra, tarot & numerology, palmistry, and allied Vedic sciences.
