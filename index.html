The Jury

To Be Announced

Jury panel details will be unveiled soon.

Jury decisions are final and binding. Jury members are recused from categories where a conflict of interest exists.

Process

1

Nomination Submission

Nominees apply online, pay the nomination fee and submit their profile, credentials, supporting documents and work samples.

2

Eligibility Screening

The internal awards team reviews submissions for completeness, eligibility and category fit before forwarding to the jury.

3

Independent Jury Scoring

Three independent expert jurors score each eligible nominee out of 100 points. Scores are submitted independently — no juror sees another's score.

4

Score Normalisation & Review

The three scores are averaged. Significant outliers are reviewed. Conflicted scores are removed and replaced.

5

Winner Announcement

The final winner list is sealed, signed off by the Awards Director and Chief Jury, and announced live on stage at the ceremony.

Evaluation Criteria

Knowledge & Expertise Impact & Contribution Client & Community Trust Digital Presence & Content Innovation & Growth Business Excellence Ethical Practice

The jury's decision is final and binding. Awards recognise excellence, contribution and integrity — not popularity or commercial volume.

Guru–Shishya Parampara

Reviving India's oldest pedagogical tradition — connecting masters of Vedic sciences with the next generation of dedicated seekers.

Why the Parampara

Deep Vedic scholarship is losing ground to short-form content — and there's no structured way for genuine masters to pass it on.

No Verification, No Trust

→

A Real Mentorship Structure

Defined cycles, limited cohorts, mutual commitment — not a course, an actual apprenticeship.

Parampara Synopsis

The Guru–Shishya Parampara at Astro+ महासंगम is a structured mentorship programme rooted in the classical Indian tradition of knowledge transfer. It connects verified Gurus — seasoned practitioners across astrology, Vastu, tarot, numerology, and palmistry — with committed Shishyas seeking authentic, in-depth learning that no app or online course can offer.

The Parampara is built for depth, not breadth. A Guru accepts a limited number of Shishyas each cycle; a Shishya commits to a defined period of structured learning. Both are verified. Both are accountable. Neither is optional.

What Makes This Different

Verified Gurus

Practitioners with a demonstrated body of work, peer recognition, and a commitment to structured knowledge transmission.

Structured Cycles

Time-bound, outcome-oriented cycles, not open-ended arrangements. Clear expectations on both sides.

Vedic Disciplines

Covering astrology, Vastu Shastra, tarot & numerology, palmistry, and allied Vedic sciences.

Mutual Selection

A Shishya applies to the Guru they want to learn from. The Guru decides whether to accept them. This isn't pay-and-enroll — it's a two-way commitment.

How It Works

1

Guru Applies

Practitioners register with their discipline, years of practice, and a short introduction.

2

Guru Is Verified

The NBT Astro team reviews credentials, body of work, and standing before listing the Guru on the parampara directory.

3

Shishya Applies to a Guru

Once Shishya registrations open, seekers browse verified Guru profiles by discipline and apply to the one they want to learn from.

4

Guru Accepts, Cycle Begins

The Guru reviews the application and decides. Once accepted, the Shishya begins the structured parampara cycle.

Who This Is For

For Gurus

You've spent years — sometimes decades — building real expertise. The Parampara gives you a verified profile on the directory, a cohort of genuinely committed students each cycle, and recognition at Astro+ महासंगम itself.
