Evaluation Steps

1

Fill the Nomination

Apply online, pay the nomination fee and submit your profile, credentials and work samples.

2

Eligibility Screening

Our third-party process partner reviews every submission for eligibility and category fit before it reaches the jury.

3

Jury Scoring

Our independent expert jurors score each nominee out of 100, separately, without seeing each other's scores.

4

Normalization

Our auditor, Knowledge Partner and Process Partner examine the scores closely and normalize them for fairness.

5

Winner Announcement

The final list is sealed, signed off by the Awards Director and Chief Jury, and announced live on stage.

Evaluation Criteria

Knowledge & Expertise Impact & Contribution Client & Community Trust Digital Presence & Content Innovation & Growth Business Excellence Ethical Practice

The jury's decision is final and binding. Awards recognise excellence, contribution and integrity, not popularity or commercial volume.

Launching on the Event Day

A structured mentorship programme rooted in the classical Indian tradition of knowledge transfer; connecting credible Gurus with committed Shishyas across Astrology, Vastu, Tarot, Numerology, Palmistry and other Mystic Sciences.

Each cycle, a Guru accepts a limited number of Shishyas. Each Shishya commits to a defined period of dedicated learning. Both are verified. Both are accountable.
